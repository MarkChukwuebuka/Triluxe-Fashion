Frequently asked questions

Will I receive the same product that I see in the picture? Read More Close

We make every effort to provide our customers with products that look the same as the images displayed on our website pages. However please keep in mind that photographs have a difficult time showing exact colors and that the actual color of a product may vary slightly from what is shown in one of our website images. We work hard to update our website descriptions and images as soon as we aware of noticeable variations in our products that customers may find unacceptable.

Where can I view my sales receipt? Read More Close

You can find your triluxe purchase receipt at the end of each purchase transaction, screenshot or print your receipts to track your orders with the Triluxe Support.

How can I return an item? Read More Close

We offer refunds to any unsatisfied customers up to 15 days after the date of the purchasing was made, no questions asked. If you would like your money to be back, simply negative to Refund then submit a refund request and explain why you are requesting this. Please note that it may take some times to receive the funds back, due to processing delays. Please note that by purchasing a product, you agree to the terms of the Refund Policy.

Where can I ship my order? Read More Close

Although we would love to receive your order, we currently cannot ship outside of the countries where our online shop is open. However, we are constantly expanding, and aim to reach even more countries in the future, for your full satisfaction!
